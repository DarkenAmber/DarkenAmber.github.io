Privacy Policy

DAscan · Last updated: March 2026
Short version: DAscan does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

DAscan does not collect any personal information. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any remote data collection.

2. Location Permission

DAscan requests Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) permission. This is required by Android to scan nearby WiFi networks and read the current network SSID.

  • Your GPS coordinates are never read, stored, or transmitted
  • The permission is used solely to enable WiFi scanning APIs
  • You can deny this permission — the app will show a guidance screen

3. WiFi Data

DAscan reads WiFi network information (SSID, BSSID, signal strength, channel, frequency) from your device's WiFi hardware. This data:

  • Is processed locally on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is not stored beyond the current app session (except last test time saved locally)

4. Local Storage

The app saves only one item to local storage: the timestamp of your last speed test. This data never leaves your device.

5. Internet Access

DAscan uses the internet connection only for the WiFi quality test — to send ping packets to Google DNS (8.8.8.8) and your local router. No personal data is included in these requests.

6. Third-Party Services

DAscan does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. There are no ads, no analytics, no crash reporting services.

7. Children's Privacy

DAscan does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts or data submission forms.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
